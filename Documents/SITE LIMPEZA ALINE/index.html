Regular Cleaning

  • Tidying, bins & mirrors
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your life

Deep Cleaning

A thorough, top-to-bottom clean designed to restore your home to its very best. Perfect for a seasonal reset, before moving in, or simply when your home deserves a little extra attention.

  • Inside oven, fridge & cupboards
  • Skirting boards, light switches & door frames
  • Limescale removal & grout cleaning
  • Behind & under furniture
  • Windows (interior) & window sills
£25 /hour

End of Tenancy Cleaning

Helping tenants and landlords leave — or receive — a property in immaculate condition. Our end of tenancy clean gives you the best possible chance of a full deposit return.

  • Full clean to agency & landlord standards
  • All rooms including hallways & storage
  • Appliances cleaned inside & out
  • Bathroom tiles, grout & fixtures
  • Ideal for pre- or post-tenancy handover
£30 /hour
